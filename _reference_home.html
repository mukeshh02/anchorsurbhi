ABOUT

Nupur Verma

Anchor & Host

A well-known emcee and fashion blogger, Nupur Verma is celebrated for her energetic and interactive personality and her ability to engage with any audience she encounters.

Nupur’s quick sense of humor, coupled with her clear understanding of various topics, has led her to acquire numerous projects as an anchor. Her confidence, great personality, and polished skills entertain the audience and drive the show forward without a dull moment.

Known for her spontaneity, grace, and oratory skills, Nupur Verma has been dazzling audiences as an intellectual, humorous, and witty emcee across a wide range of events. She has worked hard for notable brands and celebrities, accumulating vast experience in hosting over 2000+ events.

Some of the well-known events she has hosted include corporate events, weddings, receptions, award functions, central and state government events, team-building activities, private parties, birthday parties, and many more. She has also hosted events for global brands such as Adani Cement, Ambuja Cement, Kalyan Jewellers, Wallfort Heights, ACC, and REC Limited.
